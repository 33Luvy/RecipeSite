Pasta

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingredients and Equipment

  • 1lb of uncooked pasta
  • A large pot
  • Four to six quarts of cold water
  • 1-2 tablespoons of salt
  • Heat-proof spoon
  • Colander

How to make Pasta Step-by-Step

  1. Bring the water to a full, rolling boil.
  2. Cook your pasta at a rapid boil until it's tender but slightly firm to the bite.
  3. Drain the pasta in a colander.
  4. Serve pasta immediately.
